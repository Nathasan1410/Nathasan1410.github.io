Latest highlight Swara

Swara

Emergency guidance when networks fail. Built for the Gemma 4 Good Hackathon.

Offline-first Android emergency guide and AI assistant.

Swara combines a readable survival guide, on-device Gemma 4 LiteRT-LM responses, QR text sharing, and local hotspot distribution. One prepared phone can help other phones get the app, model, and guide content without depending on cloud services.
